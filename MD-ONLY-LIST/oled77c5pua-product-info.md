## Product Overview

The LG OLED77C5PUA is a 77-inch OLED evo AI TV powered by the alpha 9 AI Processor Gen8, delivering 1.7x greater neural processing power and 2.1x improved graphics over entry-level processors. With 4K resolution (3,840 x 2,160), Perfect Black certification by UL, and 100% Colour Volume + 100% Colour Fidelity verification by Intertek, this TV delivers cinema-grade picture quality.
