# LG Zero Clearance™ Counter-Depth MAX® 25 cu.ft French 3-Door Refrigerator — LF25BZH42S | LG Canada

## Product Overview

The LG LF25BZH42S is a 36-inch, 25.1 cu.ft Counter-Depth MAX® French 3-door refrigerator built around LG's Zero Clearance™ hinge system, which lets the unit sit nearly flush against a wall or cabinetry while both French doors still open fully. It ships with a factory-installed automatic ice maker, a hidden internal water dispenser, and an LED-backlit Cool Guard interior panel. Priced at $2,299.99 CAD (plus EHF) on LG.ca, it carries a 4.5/5 rating across 26 reviews, with reviewers recommending the product, and is ENERGY STAR® Certified at 632 kWh/year.

## Key Features

### Zero Clearance™ Hinge System: Full-Swing Doors Nearly Flush to the Wall
- Installs with as little as 0.16 inches (4mm) of clearance recommended between the refrigerator sides and adjacent walls or cabinetry
- Both French doors open fully even in tight kitchen layouts, without needing to pull the unit forward
- Combines with a thinner door profile for a nearly built-in look on a freestanding refrigerator

### Counter-Depth MAX®: 25.1 cu.ft in a Built-In Profile
- Delivers 25.1 cu.ft of total storage while keeping a counter-depth footprint for a flush, built-in appearance
- Product dimensions: 35 3/4" W × 70 1/4" H × 29 7/8" D
- Part of LG's Counter-Depth MAX family, which pairs full-size capacity with a slimmer install profile than standard depth refrigerators

### Elegant Thin Door Design: 43% Thinner Doors Without Losing Function
- Doors are 43% thinner than LG's previous Counter-Depth MAX model (LRYKC2606)
- Retains the same ice/water access and energy efficiency despite the slimmer profile
- Pairs with the Zero Clearance™ hinge system for a nearly seamless, built-in look

### Internal Water Dispenser with Factory-Installed Automatic Ice Maker
- Hidden internal water dispenser delivers filtered water without an exterior dispenser panel breaking up the door design
- Factory-installed automatic ice maker keeps ice on hand without manual filling
- Hybrid bar handles plus hidden pocket handles (on the freezer door) keep the exterior clean and fingerprint-resistant

### Cool Guard with Backlit Lighting & Door Cooling®+
- LED-backlit Cool Guard wall panel gives the interior a premium, pro-style look
- Door Cooling®+ vent at the top of the fresh food section extends LG's Smart Cooling system to the doors
- Helps maintain consistent temperatures from top to bottom, including in door-mounted storage

### General LG Refrigerator Support FAQ (verbatim, LG.ca Support)

*The items below are LG's standard refrigerator support FAQ — confirmed word-for-word identical (via direct DOM text extraction, not accordion screenshots) across the LF25BZH42S, LF25Z6211S, and LF21C6200S PDPs on 2026-07-22. They are general LG guidance, not specific to this SKU, so no model-specific numbers are drawn from them.*

**Q: How long does it take for my LG refrigerator to get cold after installation?**
A: After installation, allow up to 24 hours for the refrigerator to reach its set temperature. Cooling times may vary depending on room temperature, usage, and how much food is placed inside. For best results, wait until the unit has fully stabilized before loading large quantities of food.

**Q: Can I plug in my refrigerator to electrical power immediately after transporting it on its side?**
A: If the refrigerator was stored for a long period of time or moved on its side, it is necessary for the refrigerator to stand upright for 24 hours before connecting it to power.

**Q: Is it normal for my refrigerator to make noise?**
A: Yes, some sound during operation is normal. You may hear fans, compressors, or ice-making cycles as the refrigerator maintains temperature and produces ice (on models with ice makers). These sounds are part of normal operation and may vary throughout the day.

**Q: How can I adjust the temperature setting on my refrigerator?**
A: Temperature settings can be adjusted using the control panel, which may be located inside the refrigerator or on the door, depending on the model. You can also change the temperature remotely using your phone on select models equipped with ThinQ® WiFi (download the ThinQ® app and connect the fridge first). LG generally recommends maintaining a refrigerator temperature around 3°C (37°F) and a freezer temperature around -18°C (0°F) for optimal performance. Refer to your owner's manual for model-specific guidance.

**Q: How much space should I leave around the refrigerator for installation?**
A: Proper clearance is important for airflow and performance. Installation spacing requirements can vary by model. Refer to your owner's manual for detailed specifications. LG refrigerators feature Zero Clearance™ hinges on select models, designed to allow doors to open fully even in tighter installation spaces. For optimal performance, LG recommends 0.16 inches (4mm) clearance between the sides of the refrigerator and the wall.

**Q: How do I clean and maintain my refrigerator?**
A: For routine cleaning, use a soft cloth with mild detergent and warm water. Do not use abrasive cleaners or harsh chemicals. Interior shelves and bins can typically be removed for easier cleaning. Always refer to your owner's manual for recommended cleaning methods and maintenance guidelines.

**Q: Does my LG refrigerator require manual defrosting?**
A: Most LG refrigerators are equipped with an automatic defrost system, which helps prevent frost buildup and maintains efficient operation. Manual defrosting is typically not required. Refer to your owner's manual for details specific to your model.

**Q: Why isn't my refrigerator making ice or making enough ice?**
A: If your refrigerator is equipped with an ice maker, it may take up to 24 hours after installation or activation to begin producing ice (the icemaker will not begin operating until it reaches its operating temperature of –9°C or below). Ice production can be affected by factors such as freezer temperature, water supply connection, and frequency of door openings. Check that the ice maker is turned on and that the water supply is properly connected. Refer to your owner's manual for additional troubleshooting instructions.

**Q: When should I replace my water filter?**
A: It is typically recommended to replace your water filter approximately every 6 months, or as indicated by the filter replacement indicator. Refer to your owner's manual for specific instructions.

**Q: What is LG ThinQ® and how does it work with my refrigerator?**
A: On select Wi-Fi enabled models, LG ThinQ® allows you to connect your refrigerator to your smartphone. Through the app, you may be able to monitor performance, receive alerts, and adjust certain settings remotely. Features and functionality may vary by model and require a Wi-Fi connection.
