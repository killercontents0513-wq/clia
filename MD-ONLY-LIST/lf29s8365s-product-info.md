# LG French-Door Refrigerator with InstaView Door-in-Door — LF29S8365S | LG Canada

## Product Overview

The LG LF29S8365S is a 29-cubic-foot French-door refrigerator combining InstaView Door-in-Door technology with advanced ice and water dispensing capabilities. The InstaView window allows you to check contents without opening the door, saving energy while reducing cold air loss.
