## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What are the differences between the LG OLED C5 and C4?**

Gaming capabilities remain similar with 120Hz native and up to 144Hz VRR, though C5 offers improved motion handling with ClearMR 9000 certification.

**Q: What are the differences between the LG OLED C5 and G5?**
A: The G5 (flagship series) includes the alpha 11 AI Processor Gen2 (vs. alpha 9 in C5) for superior upscaling and processing. G5 features motion sensors for adaptive brightness (C5 lacks motion sensors) and may include additional gaming/processing enhancements. Both support gaming at 144Hz VRR and 0.1ms response time, but G5 targets enthusiasts requiring maximum AI capability. G5 pricing reflects flagship positioning; C5 offers excellent value as LG's best all-around performer.
