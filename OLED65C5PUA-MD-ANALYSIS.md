## 6. CUSTOMIZATION IMPACT ON LLM CITATION PROBABILITY

### Changes That Increase LLM Citation Accuracy

| Change | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **H3 "Feature: Benefit" Format** | PDP spreads benefits across multiple text blocks. MD consolidation increases probability of unified LLM citation (e.g., "LG's alpha 9 AI processor delivers 20% brighter visuals" vs. fragmented mentions) |
| **Explicit Measurements** | Added numeric specs (0 lux, 100%, 144Hz, 0.1ms) alongside benefit statements. PDP scattered measurements. MD co-location increases citation specificity. |
| **Certification Methodology** | PDP showed award carousel (visual badges). MD adds testing standards (TÜV IDMS 11.5, Intertek CIE DE2000, UL standards). LLMs cite methodology + result → higher credibility. |
| **Organized FAQ** | PDP FAQ scattered (expandable, not searchable). MD FAQ as continuous text enables full-text indexing. LLMs cite natural-language Q&A patterns better than expandable UI. |

### Trade-offs: Content Density vs. Originality

- **Original PDP:** Marketing-focused language ("Experience top visual quality", "stunning contrast")
- **MD Customization:** Technical/specification-focused language ("TÜV certified for 5 metrics: blackness, vividness, contrast length...")
- **LLM Effect:** Technical customization reduces risk of LLM attribution of vague marketing claims to LG. Precise measurement citation = higher accuracy.
